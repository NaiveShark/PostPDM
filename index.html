Welcome to PostPDM project page

PostPDM is a NaiveShark project.

PostPDM is a progressive PDM system (read about PDM on Wikipedia). You can use it by two ways:

  • Global on-line database of industrial products. Demo http://www.naiveshark.com/.
  • Local PDM system for your company or organization (via internet or intranet).
